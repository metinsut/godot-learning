# Anara Rubric: 3D Lighting Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Creative Convergence

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Luminous Balance | Hybrid baked lightmaps with real-time dynamic speculars. | 100% real-time shadows in complex, static environments. | Light is the brush; performance is the canvas. |
| VFX Integration | Volumetric fog synced to directional light shafts. | Flat lighting with no environmental depth cues. | Depth is achieved through the marriage of shadow and light. |
| GPU Optimization | Aggressive OmniLight distance culling and shadow atlas tuning. | Uncapped shadow resolution in non-critical areas. | Beauty must be sustainable. |


### 🌟 Visionary's Blueprint
Benchmark for the 'Mood' score. Lighting must define the spatial intent without starving the GPU.
