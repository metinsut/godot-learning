# Anara Rubric: Game Loop Collection Excellence Metrics

## Evolutionary Benchmark: Progressive Loops

| Criterion | Visionary Excellence (+20 Pts) | Architectural Slop (-10 Pts) | Expert Analysis (Anara's Insight) |
|:----------|:-------------------------------|:-----------------------------|:-----------------------------------|
| Rhythm | Predictable spawn intervals and feedback pulses. | Random, jerky acquisition rates. | Rhythm is the pulse of engagement. |
| Reward Logic | Escalating difficulty paired with tangible power. | Linear progress with no 'milestone' rewards. | The reward is the proof of mastery. |
| Sustainability | Low CPU-overhead for thousands of entities. | Frame-rate drops during high-count collection. | Scale is the vision. |

Focus on 'The Click'. Is the collection satisfying?
